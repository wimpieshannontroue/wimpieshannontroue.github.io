How it began

Our Story

8 years ago Shannon and Wimpie met at a cupcake fundraiser for cancer at her school. It was this day that she found out that he had some advice to offer in preparation for her university applications.

What started out as one coffee talking about studies turned into longer and more regular coffee dates where their conversations drifted from studies to getting to know each other. All this led up to the fateful day when Wimpie took Shannon out for lunch on her birthday, showed his hand with a deck of cards and the rest, as they say, is history.

Since then coffee dates became date nights, date nights became virtual date nights over the long distance and on a sunny Sunday Wimpie got down on one knee at a beautiful wine farm.

Seven years, thousands of kilometers traveled and three years of long distance, they are ready to make it official in front of everyone they love.
